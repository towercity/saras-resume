Projects

Voices of Railroad Square Art Park

Produced as my honors in the major undergraduate thesis, Voices of Railroad Square Art Park is a short documentary film that explores Railroad Square Art Park in Tallahassee, Florida. In this film I use interviews with individuals who are important to Railroad Square to contribute to a larger story of how they fit into the culture of this artist community at large.

Role: Director, Videographer, Editor

Chasing Ghosts

Have you seen this ghost? Join us on our documentary film journey to discover the meaning of these ghosts and find the creator behind it all.

Role: Director, Editor

Graham's Lemonade Stand

This video is part of a series for Tallahassee nonprofit, Emergency Care Help Organization (ECHO). Ten year old Graham has worked at a lemonade stand in his neighborhood where all profits go to ECHO to help community members in crisis situations.

Role: Videographer, Editor
